About

2020 is fast shaping up to be the year when the lives of so many of us start to go sideways. A pandemic that is not leaving us alone, a new cold war that has the potential of becoming a hot one, and an economy that might not start up again. In only a few months,the life I used to know seems to have disappeared. Like so many, being stuck at home and dealing with uncertainty about the future, I know I need to keep my mind focus on the positive. So I have decided to take myself on as a project. I am the programmer of my life. I code happiness that sticks and debug mistakes that suck. This site is a collection of things that can replanish my resolve and keep me motivated down this stretch of journey in the remaining 2020.
